Meet the Founder

Audjaneea Miller • CEO & Founder

Hello! My name is Audjaneea Miller. I am a 28-year-old established web developer based out of Denver, Colorado, and the proud founder and CEO of Ecostream.

The spark for Ecostream came from a simple, universal frustration: tirelessly asking my family members to help me lotion my back. I quickly realized that if I struggled with this daily routine, millions of others faced the exact same challenge—especially the elderly and those with limited mobility. I knew there had to be a better way, so I decided to build it.

"My mission is to bring a luxury, top-of-the-line skincare experience directly into the comfort of your home."

By blending my technical background with a passion for functional design, Ecostream is transforming daily self-care routines into effortless, high-end experiences accessible to everyone. Thank you for supporting our crowdfunding journey and helping bring this vision to life!
